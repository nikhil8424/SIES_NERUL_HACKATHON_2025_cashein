Budgeting Basics

What it is:

Budgeting is like a plan for your money. It helps you track how much you earn and spend. Saving is setting aside money for future goals.

Budgeting is the first step partner.

Key Concepts:

  • Income: Money you earn (allowance, part-time job).
  • Expenses: Money you spend (food, entertainment, transportation).
  • Needs vs. Wants: Needs are essential, wants are things you desire but don't need.
  • 50/30/20 Rule: A budgeting guideline: 50% for needs, 30% for wants, 20% for savings.
Let's go over the key concepts, shall we?

Tips:

  • Track your spending for a week.
  • Create a simple budget.
  • Set realistic savings goals.
  • Look for ways to reduce unnecessary expenses.
